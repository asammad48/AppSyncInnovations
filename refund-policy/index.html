Refund Policy

Home / Refund Policy

Last Updated: December 9, 2025

1. General Policy

At AppSync Innovations, we strive for complete client satisfaction. Our refund policy is designed to be fair to both parties while protecting the value of work completed.

2. Custom Development Projects

Due to the custom nature of development work, refunds are evaluated on a case-by-case basis. Generally, payments for completed milestones are non-refundable as they represent work already delivered.

3. Deposits

Initial project deposits are non-refundable as they cover planning, resource allocation, and initial development setup costs.

4. Requesting a Refund

To request a refund, please contact us at billing@appsyncinnovations.com with your project details and reason for the request. We will review and respond within 5 business days.

5. Refund Timeline

Approved refunds will be processed within 14 business days using the original payment method.

6. Dispute Resolution

If you're dissatisfied with our services, we encourage you to contact us first to resolve any issues. We're committed to finding a fair solution.
