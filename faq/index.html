Frequently Asked Questions

Home / FAQ
How long does a typical project take?

Project timelines vary based on complexity. A basic website takes 2-4 weeks, while custom software solutions may take 3-6 months. We provide detailed timelines during the planning phase and keep you updated throughout the development process.

What technologies do you work with?

We work with modern technologies including React, Vue.js, Node.js, Python, Django, Flutter, React Native, iOS (Swift), Android (Kotlin), AWS, Google Cloud, and more. We select the best technology stack based on your specific project requirements and business goals.

Do you offer ongoing support and maintenance?

Yes, we offer comprehensive support and maintenance packages. All projects include an initial support period, and we offer extended support plans to ensure your software remains up-to-date, secure, and performing optimally.

How do you handle project communication?

We maintain clear, consistent communication through regular status updates, weekly meetings, and a dedicated project manager. You'll have access to our project management tools to track progress in real-time.

What is your development process?

We follow an agile development methodology with iterative cycles. This includes: Discovery & Planning, Design & Prototyping, Development, Testing & QA, Deployment, and Post-Launch Support. You're involved at every stage.

Do you sign NDAs and protect intellectual property?

Absolutely. We take confidentiality seriously and are happy to sign NDAs before discussions begin. All intellectual property developed during your project belongs to you upon final payment.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept bank transfers, credit cards, PayPal, and other major payment methods. Projects are typically structured with milestone-based payments to ensure mutual satisfaction at each phase.

Can you work with our existing team?

Yes, we offer flexible engagement models including dedicated teams that can integrate with your existing staff, staff augmentation, and collaborative development approaches tailored to your needs.

Still Have Questions?

Our team is here to help. Reach out for personalized assistance.
